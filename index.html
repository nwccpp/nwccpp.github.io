Center for Communication and Public Policy
Northwestern University
Github Page

CCPP organizes and encourages research projects within Northwestern University and other institutions around the world aimed at advancing and informing public policy. Our projects and scholarship primarily fall within four policy-relevant research areas:

  • Inter-organizational Networks & Advocacy
  • Misinformation
  • Networked Democracy
  • Policy Discourse, Framing, and Opinion

For more information on our Center see https://polcomm.northwestern.edu/.
